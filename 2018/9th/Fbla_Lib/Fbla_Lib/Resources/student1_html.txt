<h1>Adding A New Student</h1>
<body>
		<p>Adding new student to the WHS Library Manager is as easy as one click
	from anywhere in the application using the Side Panel. Click on the tab <b>Users</b>
	and bring your mouse to the add a <b>new User panel</b>. Add the information needed and
	generate a new id or add an old id. Select Student at the bottom radio buttons and Click Add.</p>
