<h1> Adding A New Book </h1>
<body>
	<p>Adding a new book to the WHS Library Manager is as easy as one click
	from anywhere in the application using the <b>Side Panel</b>. Click on the tab <b>Books</b>
	and bring your mouse to the add a <b>new book panel</b>. Add the information needed and
	generate a new id or add the id on your book. Click the Add Button and you have added a new Book!</p>
